What is a banana trap? In a grocery store most of the customers will buy bananas. If someone buys a razor and a banana then you cannot tell that the purchase of a razor influenced the purchase of banana. Hence we need to adjust the formula to handle this case as well. The modified version is (Total People who watched X and Y / Total People who watched X) / (Total People who did not watch X but watched Y / Total People who did not watch X) We have the numberator, we just need to calculate the denominator.
